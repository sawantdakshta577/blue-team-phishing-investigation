# Phishing Email Investigation #2

## Original Email

From: [hr@careers-google.net](mailto:hr@careers-google.net)

Subject: Interview Selection Notice

Congratulations!

You have been selected for an interview at Google.

To schedule your interview and receive your offer letter, download the attached file and enter your personal details.

Regards,
Google HR Team

---

## Alert Summary

The email claims that the recipient has been selected for a Google interview and requests downloading an attachment and submitting personal information.

## Sender Analysis

The sender uses the domain careers-google.net, which is not an official Google domain. The domain should be treated as suspicious until verified.

## Attachment Analysis

The email encourages the recipient to download an attachment. Phishing emails often use malicious attachments to deliver malware or steal information.

## Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)

* Sender: [hr@careers-google.net](mailto:hr@careers-google.net)
* Suspicious domain: careers-google.net
* Unexpected attachment
* Request for personal information

## Social Engineering Tactics

* Uses excitement and opportunity ("selected for an interview").
* Impersonates a trusted company.
* Encourages immediate action.
* Requests sensitive personal information.

## Verdict

Potential Phishing Email (True Positive)

## Risk Level

High

## Recommendation

* Do not open the attachment.
* Verify the opportunity through official Google careers channels.
* Report the email to the security team.
* Block the sender if confirmed malicious.
